About Sludgers

Sludgers is a community-forward initiative with a simple mission: restore and protect local green spaces using low-tech and high-heart solutions. We combine planting drives, habitat-friendly landscaping, and public education with a soft, futuristic aesthetic — because caring for nature is also about imagining kinder futures.
